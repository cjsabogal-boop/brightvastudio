1. INTRODUCTION

Paula Blanco, doing business as BrightVA Studio ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

We implement commercially reasonable security measures designed to protect your information. For client credentials (passwords), we use encrypted password management tools (e.g., LastPass/1Password).
