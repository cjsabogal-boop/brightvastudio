Services

Strategic Business Solutions

01

Executive Operations & Management

Ideal for CEOs, Founders, and Busy Executives who need to reclaim their time.

Strategic Calendar Management

Stop playing Tetris with your time. I don't just schedule meetings; I protect your focus. I manage complex time zones, set up buffer times to prevent burnout, and handle rescheduling seamlessly. I ensure your calendar reflects your business priorities, not just other people's demands.

Inbox Triage & Communication

Reach 'Inbox Zero' without the stress. I filter noise, highlight urgent matters, and draft professional responses on your behalf. Whether it's negotiating with vendors or replying to clients, I maintain your professional voice in both English and Spanish.

Executive Travel Logistics

Travel with the ease of a diplomat. Leveraging my background in embassy logistics, I coordinate end-to-end travel arrangements: flights, premium accommodation, ground transport, and detailed visual itineraries. I anticipate problems before they happen so you can focus on the destination, not the journey.

Bilingual Business Liaison

Break down language barriers to expand your business. I act as your bridge for operations in Latin America and the US/Canada. From translating documents and interpreting during virtual meetings to managing relationships with Spanish-speaking stakeholders, I ensure nothing gets lost in translation.

02

Creative Studio & Social Media

Ideal for brands that want to look professional, aesthetic, and consistent.

Social Media Curation & Community

Your digital storefront should look as professional as you are. I handle the day-to-day of your social presence: curating aesthetic content, designing on-brand posts, scheduling for optimal times, and engaging with your audience to turn followers into loyal clients.

High-Impact Presentation Design

Ditch the boring slides. I transform your rough notes and data into sleek, persuasive Executive Pitch Decks, Sales Presentations, and webinar slides. I combine corporate structure with creative flair to keep your audience engaged.

Digital Asset Creation

Professional collateral that sells for you. I design Lead Magnets (E-books), Media Kits, One-Pagers, and Welcome Packets that align perfectly with your brand identity, helping you position yourself as an authority in your niche.

Newsletter & Email Marketing

Stay top-of-mind with your clients. I format, design, and schedule your weekly or monthly newsletters. I ensure your list is segmented correctly and your emails look perfect on mobile and desktop, ready to drive clicks and sales.

03

Lifestyle & Special Projects

For the entrepreneur who needs support beyond the boardroom.

Personal Lifestyle Concierge

Success requires balance. Let me handle the personal details that distract you from the big picture: making restaurant reservations, researching gifts for clients/family, booking medical appointments, or managing household vendors.

Virtual Event & Webinar Coordination

Host flawless online events. From setting up the Zoom/Webinar backend and managing registrations to sending reminders and providing live technical support, I ensure your virtual events run smoothly from start to finish.
